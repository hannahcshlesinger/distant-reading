What is Distant Reading?

This website is a project developed for Digital Humanities Archival Course, a course taught by Dr Adam Anderson in Summer 2021 at UC Berkeley. This course is part of the requirements for UC Berkeleyâ€™s Digital Humanities Summer Minor and incorporates projects from other DH summer courses. The Digital Humanities minor places strong emphasis on computational text analysis methods, which is why I decided to create a website dedicated to the topic. The goal of the site is familiarize visitors with the basic concepts of distant reading and computational text analysis.
